About Wabebe

Revolutionizing fleet management across East Africa

Our Story

Wabebe was born from a simple observation: fleet management in East Africa was broken. Vehicle owners were losing money to inefficient operations, drivers lacked the tools to succeed, and transport companies struggled with outdated systems.

Founded by Fred Njuguna and Mohammed Hussein, two entrepreneurs with deep roots in Kenya's transport industry, Wabebe set out to change this. We saw matatu owners manually tracking collections on paper, SACCO managers juggling spreadsheets, and drivers navigating without proper support.

Today, we're building the platform that puts every fleet owner—from single-vehicle operators to large transport companies—in control of their operations.
